Android Development

I publish apps to Google Play under the developer name Rhino Development I currently have two apps in the store, and a custom Asteroids game that I hope to publish soon.
SAC Sphero Labyrinth

I wrote this app for the Strategic Air Command & Aerospace Museum to use for education purposes. The app connects to a Sphero robot via bluetooth, and allows the user to send direction commands to navigate the Sphero through a maze.

Foscam Control Center

My first attempt at a full featured Android application, I wrote Foscam Control Center to control older model Foscam IP Cameras because I found the other available Android applications clunky and hard to use. This was a successful app for older model Foscams when it I wrote it several years ago, but with many new models coming out, this app is more or less obsolete, and no longer being maintained.
